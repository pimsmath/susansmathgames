Stores

Some good sources of mathematical/logical games & puzzles
Recommended by Susan

  • Craving for a Game in Central City shopping centre in Surrey;  a good selection of puzzle-type games and a huge selection of board games

  • Dufferin Games Room in Abbotsford; a fair selection of puzzle-type games, as well as many board games

  • Mastermind Toys in Langley and in Abbotsford; these have a moderate selection of games; on-line ordering as well

  • Science World in Vancouver usually has a good selection of some newer games

  • Secret Garden Toys in Nelson: a good selection, knowledgeable staff**
    **

Recommended by other people (I haven’t had the chance to visit these yet)

  • Discovery Toys:  has an on-line catalogue, seems to be mostly for the younger set; check out the offerings for “families/groups”

  • Mind Games at Metrotown (Burnaby) and Coquitlam Centre (Coquitlam); on-line ordering as well

  • Village Toy Shop in Newport Village, Port Moody

Please send me your recommendations or add them below.
